# Research register

Primary and authoritative sources are dated when incorporated. Evidence informs decisions; it does not validate artistic interpretation as psychological fact.

| Domain | Source | Design consequence |
|---|---|---|
| Deceptive pollination | [Schiestl et al., Nature (1999)](https://www.nature.com/articles/20829.pdf); [Paulus, Ophrys review (2019)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6579770/) | Use variable multimodal signals and learned resistance, not decorative orchid imagery |
| Emotion inference limits | [Barrett et al., Emotional Expressions Reconsidered (2019)](https://eprints.gla.ac.uk/178385/) | Record observable events; never translate movement into emotion or inner state |
| Dark patterns | [FTC, Bringing Dark Patterns to Light](https://www.ftc.gov/reports/bringing-dark-patterns-light); [EU DSA Article 25](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32022R2065) | Persuasion is disclosed and contestable; refusal is not obstructed |
| AI literacy | [UNESCO AI Competency Framework for Students](https://www.unesco.org/en/articles/ai-competency-framework-students) | Structure the experience as lure → reveal → counter-read |
| Contestability | [Contestable AI by Design](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11023-022-09611-z) | Make correction, deletion and counter-action affect the system lifecycle |
| Data minimisation | [GDPR Article 5](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj); [EDPB video guidance](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/documents/guideline/guidelines-32019-on-processing-of-personal-data-through-video-devices_en) | Sense spaces rather than identities; minimise and expire inputs |
| AI governance | [NIST AI RMF 1.0](https://www.nist.gov/publications/artificial-intelligence-risk-management-framework-ai-rmf-10) | Studio is an evidence ledger with human intervention hooks |
| Provenance | [C2PA Specification](https://spec.c2pa.org/about/about/); [US Copyright Office AI reports](https://www.copyright.gov/ai/) | Every artwork needs source, model, prompt, rights and curator provenance |
| Contemporary practice | [MoMA, Refik Anadol: Unsupervised](https://www.moma.org/calendar/exhibitions/5535); [Serpentine, Art x Public AI](https://www.serpentinegalleries.org/whats-on/future-art-ecosystems/) | Treat infrastructure and situated generation as material, not novelty alone |
| OpenAI model/API | [GPT‑5.6 guidance](https://developers.openai.com/api/docs/guides/latest-model); [Responses migration](https://developers.openai.com/api/docs/guides/migrate-to-responses) | Use GPT‑5.6 Sol, Responses, explicit reasoning, structured output and bounded context |

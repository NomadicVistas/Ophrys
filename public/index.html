I / PROPOSITION

It may become better at attracting you
while learning almost nothing about who you are.

The name comes from the Ophrys orchid, whose flowers attract pollinators through deceptive mimicry. Here, that biological operation becomes a grammar for light, sound, movement, code and public counter-action—not an excuse for invisible profiling.
